Application procedure

  • To become a Geekhub student one should follow 4 steps:

    1 step Apply to the selected course via our website geekhub.ck.ua
    2 step Attend introductory meeting to get all required info about courses, exams, etc.
    3 step Pass written exams.
    4 step Pass the interview (for people who successfully passed the exams).

  • What should I know to pass entrance exams?

    At the beginning of August we will provide the main topics which one should know to pass the exam. Each course has its own list of topics. This approach provides unexperienced but motivated people an opportunity to pass the exam successfully.

  • How much should I pay for the course?

    GeekHub is a community, that’s why courses are free. We require from our potential students the high level of motivations and a lot of self-study at home.

  • Can GeekHub help me to find a job?

    We don’t provide employment for cource leavers, but Cherkassy companies know the high level of Geekhub teachers and certified students, and apply to us for recommendations. More than 80 percent of certificated students have been employed after graduation.
