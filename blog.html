Pleasantly surprised

February 29, 2012

When planning Geekhub, we compiled a program for PHP and frontend students that will take them from zero to a junior level developer. The program intended to take small steps in order for everyone to follow.

After launch, during preliminary courses we started to get a sense that a lot of these guys already had a good understanding of the basics of programming and web application structure. Some of them came up to us asking to get a more advanced level taught to them. As a result we had to amend the program to include OOP and frameworks right from the get go.

All of the Geekhub teachers are telling me they are surprised at the skill level of their students. We didn’t expect this and are happy to assist them to go further. Such motivation to learn is inspiring and reassures us that Geekhub is a grand idea after all.

Yuri Kurat
