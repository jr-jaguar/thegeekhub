  • The Team
  • GeekHub is a project that enables talented young people to gain practical knowledge and skills in software development and IT in general. The Geekhub team is made up of a group of professionals in information technology, which has a singular goal of preparing students for a successful career in the software development industry. All team members of Geekhub are currently working in leading software development companies, and are updating their knowledge and skills in information technology on a daily basis. This setup enables the GeekHub team to teach only the skills that are relevant and needed in the production of real world software development projects.
  • Geekhub is actively looking for motivated university students who are dreaming about launching a successful career in information technology. It is our intention that young talent, can join a community of IT professionals for intense practical training in web and mobile app development and as a result, create hundreds of unique integrated software products for the international business community.
  • We call Geekhub “Open Source”, because it’s the idea of “Open” that directs the program. Everything we do revolves around open relationships between people. Geekhub is open for anyone to apply and is 100% free of charge. All that we require is that students be “open” to a lot of hard work, internal motivation and practice at home!
